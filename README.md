A neural circuit simulator made with NeutralinoJS. Made for Hack Club Flavortown YSWS. Frontend in HTML-JS, backend in C++.
Place down neurons and connect them with various types of synapses, add stimulation and start!

No need to press the 'Reset' button to restart with an empty canvas, just Ctrl+r to reload it, since it's WebView.

Only tested on 64-bit Windows. May or may not work on Linux.
